About PistonPals

We’re a fan project that helps players repair broken or corrupted saves in My Summer Car. Whether your engine’s missing or your car’s possessed, we’ll take a look and do our best to bring it back.

How It Works

  1. Upload your save and describe the problem.
  2. We review and repair it.
  3. You get a response with your fixed save or next steps.

What We Fix

  • ✅ Engine missing or messed up
  • ✅ Car won’t start or drive
  • ✅ Wiring or assembly problems
  • ❌ We do not install game mods. Only Satsuma mods.
