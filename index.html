About PistonPals

We’re a fan project that helps players repair their broken Satsuma's engine in My Summer Car. Whether your engine’s missing or your car’s possessed, we’ll take a look and do our best to bring it back.

How It Works

  1. Upload your save and describe the problem.
  2. Contact us if you want to add details.
  3. We review and repair it.
  4. You get a response with your fixed save or next steps.

ℹ️ More information for this site

This is a site made with the idea for helping players by fixing their Satsuma's for them. If you do not know what My Summer Car is, it is an Unity based game about mechanicing. You are an 19 year old in 1995, which lives in Finland. The goal of the game... Is to suffer. And to make a rally car :0 Yes. The developer made the game with the idea of suffering, and Finland's love of rally racing. You have: ;thirst ;hunger ;stress ;urine. You're parents have left to an island named Tenerife on vacation. Without you. And because it is 1995, there is no Instagram, no Tiktok, or any social medias, So the only time to pass the time is build your father's old car. A Datsun 100-A. (called Satsuma in the game) From the bare. Chassis. Yes, it will be hard work, but atleast there will be an sense of happines or pride when the car starts.

FAQ

How long does it take?
It depends. If you need a rebuild, it will take 1 to 2 hours. If it is an easy fix, it takes 20–55 minutes.

Will I lose progress?
No. We aim to keep everything the same unless you ask for something changed.

Are the services free?
Yes, because this is a fan-made project.
