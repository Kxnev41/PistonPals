About PistonPals

PistonPals is a fan project made with the idea for the people behind PistonPals to help people by fixing their Satsumas for them. Whether your Satsuma is broken or possessed, we will patch it up and put you back on the road.

How It Works

  1. Upload your save, then give us some details.
  2. Contact us if you would like to provide more details.
